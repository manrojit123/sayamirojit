THE BENIFITS

CBRS BENIFITS

COST

CBRS Private LTE cheaper long term than WiFi given range, scalability, and ecosystem benefits

DATA CAPACITY

CBRS Private LTE can support up to 2x-3x data capacity of WiFi due to lack of interference

QUALITY OF SERVICE

CBRS Private LTE can differentiate /prioritize QoS based on users and traffic characteristics (typical in LTE)

INTERFERENCE

CBRS Private LTE can support up to 7000 sq ft coverage area per AP whereas WiFi < 3000 sq ft

SECURITY

CBRS Private LTE offers greater security compared to WiFi since spectrum is “controlled” by SaS

MOBILITY

CBRS Private LTE has handoffand roaming mechanisms, whereas WiFi does not
